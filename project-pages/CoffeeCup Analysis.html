CoffeeCup Dataset
The Analysis is prepared for Business Assessment Class to analyze the company's profile with the previous year's dataset. The given dataset is first cleaned and prepared for analysis with OpenRefine
Project Details
This report has been prepared to present the analysis of the data of The Coffee Cup for 2018 and 2019. The information provided within the data and its quality and gaps have been summarized to provide supporting evidence on the developed graphs.
It can be observed that the given data is inadequate to provide a clear roadmap and strategic planning for the next years. Considering the gaps detailed above, there needs to be a detailed survey and study conducted not just on financial details but also on customer information and satisfaction, employee satisfaction, training and turnover, product details including beverages, food and snacks, and store operations and asset assessment. All collected information will need to be matched with the financials of the company and put together as a Business Strategy Plan to meet the company's performance metrics, core values and most importantly the company's mission statement.
